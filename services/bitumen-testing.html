RM Associates offers advanced bitumen testing services to assess the physical and rheological properties of paving grade bitumen. Our testing ensures material quality, performance, and compliance with Indian Standards (IS).

Our Bitumen Testing Capabilities

Delivering accurate material evaluation through standardized testing methods.

Penetration Test

Determines hardness and consistency of bitumen to assess its suitability for road construction.

Softening Point Test

Identifies the temperature at which bitumen softens to evaluate performance under heat.

Viscosity Test

Determines flow behavior at different temperatures for proper mixing and laying.

Flash & Fire Point Test

Assesses safety during heating operations by identifying ignition temperature.
