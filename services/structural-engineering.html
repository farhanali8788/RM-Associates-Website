Buildings

We provide structural inspection, stability certification, and performance evaluation services for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Our assessments ensure structural safety, load capacity adequacy, and long-term durability.

The evaluation includes visual inspection, structural analysis, material testing review, and compliance verification with relevant Indian Standards.

Key Features

  • Structural stability assessment
  • Load carrying capacity evaluation
  • Crack and distress analysis
  • Renovation & retrofitting recommendations
  • Safety certification for statutory approval

Equipment Used

  • Rebound Hammer
  • Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) Equipment
  • Crack Width Gauge
  • Digital Theodolite / Total Station
  • Structural Analysis Software
Standards Followed: IS 456, IS 875, IS 800, IS 15988

Railway Over Bridge (ROBC)

Our ROBC services include inspection, structural evaluation, and technical assessment of railway over bridges to ensure safe operation under dynamic traffic and railway loading conditions.

We analyze superstructure, substructure, bearings, piers, abutments, and expansion joints to verify performance and structural integrity.

Key Features

  • Dynamic load assessment
  • Bearing & expansion joint inspection
  • Deck slab condition analysis
  • Foundation and pier evaluation
  • Compliance with IRC & Railway standards

Equipment Used

  • UPV Testing Equipment
  • Rebound Hammer
  • Strain Measuring Instruments
  • Crack Monitoring Sensors
  • Total Station for Alignment Check
Standards Followed: IRC Codes, IRS Bridge Rules, IS 456

Bridge

We conduct comprehensive bridge inspection and structural health assessment services for highway and infrastructure bridges.

The evaluation ensures structural safety, load adequacy, durability, and preventive maintenance planning.

Key Features

  • Superstructure & substructure inspection
  • Load rating analysis
  • Corrosion & fatigue assessment
  • Deck condition evaluation
  • Maintenance & rehabilitation planning

Equipment Used

  • Rebound Hammer
  • UPV Testing Device
  • Core Cutting Equipment
  • Alignment Survey Instruments
  • Bridge Inspection Tools
Standards Followed: IRC SP 35, IRC 112, IS 456

Road

RM Associates provides structural evaluation and inspection services for flexible and rigid pavements, highways, and urban road networks.

Our services help determine pavement strength, surface condition, and performance under traffic loads.

Key Features

  • Pavement condition assessment
  • Surface distress evaluation
  • Structural capacity verification
  • Rutting & cracking analysis
  • Rehabilitation strategy planning

Equipment Used

  • Falling Weight Deflectometer (FWD)
  • Core Cutting Machine
  • Benkelman Beam
  • Traffic Counting Equipment
  • Total Station for Road Alignment
Standards Followed: IRC 37, IRC 81, MORTH Specifications
