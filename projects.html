Western Coalfields Limited – Survey Investigation

RM Associates carried out comprehensive surveying and contour mapping services for Western Coalfields Limited to support planning and development activities within the project area. The work involved detailed topographical surveys to capture accurate ground levels, terrain features, and site conditions.

Using modern surveying equipment and precise field measurement techniques, the team generated high-resolution contour maps and detailed site data required for engineering design, planning, and infrastructure development. The survey ensured reliable spatial information that assists in project feasibility, land assessment, and future development planning.

The final deliverables included accurate topographical data, contour mapping, and survey reports, enabling Western Coalfields Limited to make informed decisions for efficient project execution.

ClientWestern Coalfields Limited
ScopeGeotechnical Investigation
LocationMaharashtra
Year2024

ATCC Traffic Survey – Airports Authority of India

RM Associates carried out Automatic Traffic Counter & Classifier (ATCC) data collection to analyze traffic movement and vehicle classification near the Airports Authority of India project area. The study involved systematic monitoring and recording of vehicular traffic to support transportation planning and infrastructure assessment.

Advanced traffic monitoring equipment was deployed to capture vehicle count, classification, and movement patterns over a defined observation period. The collected data was analyzed to understand traffic volume, peak-hour patterns, and road usage trends.

The findings helped provide reliable traffic statistics and insights required for efficient infrastructure planning, traffic management, and future development around the airport zone.

ClientAirports Authority Of India
ScopeATCC Traffic Data Collection & Analysis
LocationNagpur
Year2018

Avaada Group - Non Destructive testing

RM Associates carried out Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services for Avaada Group to assess the quality and structural integrity of construction materials and components without causing any damage. The testing was conducted to ensure that the structures meet required engineering and safety standards.

The scope included performing various NDT methods to evaluate material strength, detect internal defects, and verify construction quality during project execution. The inspection and analysis helped maintain high standards of quality assurance and structural reliability for the project.

The results provided valuable insights for engineers and project managers, supporting safe, durable, and compliant infrastructure development.

ClientAvaada Group
ScopeNon-Destructive Testing (NDT)
LocationNagpur
Year2023

PWD – NSV (Network Survey Vehicle)

RM Associates carried out a Network Survey Vehicle (NSV) survey for the Public Works Department (PWD) to assess the condition and performance of the existing road network. The survey involved collecting high-precision road data using advanced vehicle-mounted survey equipment.

The NSV system was used to capture road surface conditions, pavement distress, roughness, and geometric characteristics of the roadway. The collected data was processed and analyzed to provide accurate insights into road quality and maintenance requirements.

The study supported the Public Works Department in road asset management, infrastructure planning, and maintenance decision-making, ensuring safer and more efficient road networks.

ClientPublic Works Department (PWD)
Scope NSV (Network Survey Vehicle)
LocationNagpur, Maharashtra
Year2023

Maha Metro – DGPS survey

RM Associates carried out a Differential Global Positioning System (DGPS) survey for Maha Metro to support planning and development of metro infrastructure. The survey was conducted to obtain high-precision geographic coordinates and accurate ground control data required for engineering design and project implementation.

Using advanced DGPS equipment and surveying techniques, the team captured precise positional data, control points, and geospatial measurements across the project area. The collected data helped establish reliable reference points for mapping, alignment planning, and infrastructure development.

The survey provided accurate spatial information and mapping support, enabling engineers and planners to carry out metro infrastructure development with improved precision and efficiency.

ClientMaha Metro
ScopeDGPS Survey
LocationMaharashtra
Year2024
